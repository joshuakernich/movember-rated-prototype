The Aggressive Man

You notice your friend from class has been missing quite a few classes and failed his midterm exam. He also seemed to be more withdrawn than normal when you last saw him in class.

You reach out to him and he sends a rambling text message that is aggressive and defensive.

  • Hey mate, How are you?

  • Oh, so now you're interested!?

  • What do you mean?

  • How about not ignoring me for weeks because things are going so well for you.


    I see you posting about all the cool things you've been up to but haven't invited me once.


    And NOW you ask how I am!?



Good Listening

Good listening is more than just hearing what is being said. Good listening is understanding and identifying a need or emotion. Good listening is the basis of a healthy conversation.

Good Listeners

Good listeners not only focus on the voice, but body language, tone and context of the conversation.

Good listeners avoid making assumptions or jumping to conclusions.

Good listeners do not have to provide answers or solutions.

Good listeners do not make judgement.

Good listeners ask clarifying questions to ensure they understand.
